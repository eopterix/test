For easy access just do a port forward:
    kubectl port-forward svc/flowset-control-app-service {{ .Values.flowset.control.server.port }}:8081

You can then access the web interface on:
    https://{{ $ws }}8081/login 

Without the "/login" part you are redirected to your Datalab for some reason.
